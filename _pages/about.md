Welcome
This lab focuses on systems modeling and algorithm research for applications characterized by collective optimization where decision makers cooperatively optimize the system under the partial information sharing.

This lab involves both governmental and industry funded projects dealing with developing decentralized planning, scheduling and control in smart factory, railway system and supply chain system.
